Watch L8R

3rd-party Youtube Client Expanding Watch Later Functionality

Previous Next

This project, completed as a group project for the CSCI 3308 final, provides users with a streamlined way to manage their Youtube Watch Later list. Using the Youtube API, we even allow users to search Youtube from inside our site, so they don't have to switch between youtube.com and our project. Unintentionally, it also doesn't show ads. Google please don't sue us.
